# Reflection: Profile Comparisons

## Happy Pop vs Chill Lofi
These two felt like night and day. Pop gave me upbeat songs like Sunrise 
City, Lofi flipped it completely to Midnight Coding and Library Rain. 
Makes sense. The energy went from 0.8 down to 0.35 and the genre 
changed. Crazy how just changing two numbers in the profile switches 
the whole vibe.

## Happy Pop vs Deep Intense Rock
Both want high energy but that's where the similarity ends. Pop got 
Sunrise City, Rock got Storm Runner. The funny part was Gym Hero 
showing up in the rock results. It's a pop song but its energy was 
close enough to sneak in. That showed me genre is strong but energy 
can still pull in songs that don't fully belong.

## Chill Lofi vs Deep Intense Rock
Total opposites. Lofi wants chill, low energy, acoustic. Rock wants 
intense, high energy, no acoustics. The results had zero songs in 
common which honestly was satisfying. It means the system actually 
knows the difference between two very different users.
